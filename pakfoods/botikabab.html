Chicken Boti Kabab

Pakistani chicken boti kabab is a culinary delight that exemplifies the vibrant flavors of the region. This dish features succulent pieces of marinated chicken, meticulously seasoned with a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, ginger, garlic, and chili, combined with yogurt for a tenderizing and flavor-enhancing marinade. The marinated chicken is then skewered and grilled to perfection, resulting in juicy, smoky, and perfectly charred morsels bursting with aromatic spices. The balance of tangy yogurt, earthy spices, and the tenderness of the chicken creates a symphony of flavors that dance on the palate. Served with a side of mint chutney, fresh naan, and zesty salad, Pakistani chicken boti kabab is a culinary masterpiece cherished for its enticing taste and cultural significance in Pakistani cuisine.

The recipe of Pakistani yummy & Tasty chicken boti kabab:

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Skewers, soaked in water (if using wooden skewers)
  • Chopped cilantro and lemon wedges for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and oil. Mix well to form a marinade.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and coat them thoroughly. Cover the bowl and marinate the chicken in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight, to let the flavors infuse.
  3. Preheat your grill or barbecue to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the skewers, leaving a little space between each piece for even cooking.
  5. Grease the grill grates lightly with oil to prevent sticking. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, or until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice charred exterior.
  6. Once the chicken boti kababs are cooked, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a couple of minutes.
  7. Serve the chicken boti kababs hot, garnished with chopped cilantro and lemon wedges if desired. They can be enjoyed with naan, roti, or accompanied by a fresh salad and chutney.

Serve hot with naan bread or steamed rice.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Pakistani chicken boti kababs!
